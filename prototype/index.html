Plan Your Trip

Arriving

Most visitors arrive by air. The airport currently supports small jets and prop planes and is expanding.

Money & Payments

Currency: USD; many accept euros and yen; major credit cards widely accepted. Banks offer currency exchange.

Health & Safety

One hospital and several clinics; many multilingual staff. Violent crime is rare; use standard precautions.

FAQs

What are alcohol sales hours? Alcohol may not be served or sold between 12:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m.
What is the drinking age? The drinking age is 18 and is not strictly enforced.
Will my electronics work? Power outlets are 120V (U.S. standard).
Are places open on holidays? Taniti observes many holidays; some attractions/restaurants may be closed. Plan accordingly.
Is English spoken? Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English; less common in rural areas and with older residents.
