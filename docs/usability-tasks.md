# Objective Usability Tasks (Task 2)

Use the interactive prototype URL. Ask users to think aloud and mark success/failure and time on task.

1. Find bus service hours for Taniti City.
- Expected path: Home → Getting Around → “Public Buses (5 a.m.–11 p.m.)”.

2. Identify at least two adventure activities and how to book them.
- Expected path: Explore Attractions → Zip-lining / Volcano tour cards → “Book via partner” links.

3. Confirm alcohol sales hours and the drinking age on Taniti.
- Expected path: Plan Your Trip → FAQs → Alcohol hours / Drinking age.

4. Locate government‑regulated lodging options and where to check availability.
- Expected path: Stay → Hotels/B&Bs/Resort overview → provider links.

5. Verify currency and payment options accepted on the island.
- Expected path: Plan Your Trip → Money & Payments → USD, euros, yen; major credit cards.
